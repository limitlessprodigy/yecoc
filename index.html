Our Mission

To provide entrepreneurial resources for entrepreneurs promoting education, camaraderie, community involvement, access to resources and an opportunity for growth.

We will support entrepreneurs through all stages of business development. Our organization is made to connect businesses to each other, to prospective customers and to essential resources. We facilitate connections that lead to long-term business relationships built on trust.

Our Vision
For The Community

By advancing innovative solutions that create jobs, increase local investment and enhance our region, the Young Entrepreneurs Chamber of Commerce will be a catalyst for business prosperity.

The Chamber will foster a vibrant and interconnected business community where collaboration increases capacity, education enhances ability and advocacy influences authority.

Our Entrepreneural
Values

We value the entrepreneurial spirit of a leveled playing field. Where everyone has an opportunity to succeed. We work to create a business climate that attracts new investment and enhances growth and expansion opportunities for existing companies as well as new startup companies.

In the Young Entrepreneurs Chamber Of Commerce we promote innovation and tradition as well as diversity and lifelong learning.
